About Aqsa International Quran Academy

Aqsa International Quran Academy is an online Quran learning academy dedicated to helping children and adults learn the Holy Quran from the comfort of their homes. Our qualified and experienced teachers provide one-to-one online Quran classes with proper Tajweed and pronunciation. We welcome male and female students from all around the world and offer flexible class timings according to the student's convenience.
